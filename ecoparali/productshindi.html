COMPOST & SOIL
AMENDMENT

Stubble can be incorporated into compost piles or it can be directly applied to the fields as a soil amendment. As the stubble decomposes, it releases nutrients into the soil, that further improves soil structure and fertility.

कम्पोस्ट और मृदा
संशोधन

स्टबल को कम्पोस्ट स्टेकों में शामिल किया जा सकता है या यह सीधे खेतों में मृदा संशोधन के रूप में लागू किया जा सकता है। स्टबल का अपचय होते ही, यह मृदा में पोषक तत्वों को मुक्त करता है, जो मृदा संरचना और उर्वरता को और भी बेहतर बनाता है।

EROSION
CONTROL

Stubble can be left on the fields as a protective cover to reduce soil erosion. By retaining moisture of the soil and preventing wind and water erosion, stubble helps in maintaining soil health and fertility. Stubble can be formed into erosion control mats that can be directly used on fields.

PAPER &
PULP

Stubble fibers can be processed into paper and pulp products. Incorporating stubble into paper production reduces the demand for wood-based pulp and supports sustainable forestry practices, reducing potential deforestation.

Read More

TEXTILE
MANUFACTURING

Stubble fibers can be spun into yarn and used in textile manufacturing. Products made from stubble fibers offer a renewable and biodegradable alternative to conventional textiles. The products manufactured as such are much light in weight and provide a better form of comfort.
